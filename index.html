First-Time Flood?
Here’s What to Expect

For most situations, the restoration process includes two phases.

Phase 1

Mitigation

This includes demolition, disinfection and drying. Usually, this takes 3-5 days.

Phase 2

Reconstruction

During reconstruction, we’ll repair and replace the water damage so your home feels like new.

Depending on the severity of the water damage, this process takes anywhere from a few days to a few months.
